Strategic Partnership September 1, 2025

Strategic Partnership with Fivetran

We're thrilled to announce our strategic partnership with Fivetran, which improves how teams approach Marketing Mix Modeling by removing the data preparation bottleneck.

This Integration Makes Things Much Easier

Fivetran's ad_reporting dbt package brings together spend, impressions, clicks, and conversion metrics from major channels (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, Amazon) into a unified format. PyMC-Marketing now transforms those standardized tables into production-grade Bayesian MMM models.

What We've Built: New data loaders including process_fivetran_ad_reporting() and process_fivetran_shopify_unique_orders() that transform Fivetran/dbt outputs into MMM-ready inputs in just a few lines of code.

Real Impact: Teams report moving from weeks of data wrangling to days for complete MMM deployment. This partnership enables data teams to focus on insights and decision-making rather than ETL complexity.
