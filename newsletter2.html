Partnership

Strategic Partnership with Fivetran

Announced September 1, 2025

We're excited to announce our strategic partnership with Fivetran, which makes Marketing Mix Modeling much easier by removing the data preparation bottleneck.

This Integration Makes It Much Easier

Fivetran's ad_reporting dbt package brings together spend, impressions, clicks, and conversion metrics from major channels into a unified format. PyMC-Marketing now turns those standardized tables into production-grade Bayesian MMM models.

What We Shipped: New data loaders including process_fivetran_ad_reporting() and process_fivetran_shopify_orders_unique_orders() that transform Fivetran/dbt outputs into MMM-ready inputs in just a few lines of code.

The Impact: Teams report moving from weeks of data wrangling to days for complete MMM deployment. This partnership enables data teams to focus on insights and decision-making rather than ETL complexity.

Release

PyMC-Marketing 0.16.0

Released September 1, 2025
PyMC-Marketing 0.16.0 Release Banner
New features: Fivetran Data Loaders, Sensitivity Analysis, HSGP Support

Our latest PyMC-Marketing release brings powerful new features that enhance your Marketing Mix Modeling capabilities and streamline your workflow.

Major New Features

  • Fivetran Data Loaders: Seamless integration with Fivetran's data pipeline
  • Sensitivity Analysis: Enhanced marginal effects capabilities
  • HSGP Support: Time-varying media and intercept modeling
  • Enhanced ModelBuilder: Rich table displays and better indexing

This release represents months of collaboration with our enterprise clients and community feedback. The Fivetran integration alone reduces data preparation time from weeks to minutes.

Contributors: Special thanks to our 10 contributors who made this release possible, including new contributors @daniel-saunders-phil and @aaron1-z.
